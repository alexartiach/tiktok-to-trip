The Problem: Inspiration to Action Gap

Gen Z and Millennials discover amazing travel content daily, but turning it into real trips is a nightmare.

🕰

6+ Hours of Manual Planning

43% of travelers say creating itineraries is the most time-consuming part of trip planning. Manually researching each location mentioned takes forever.

🔍

Lost in Translation

That amazing restaurant from the TikTok? Good luck finding the exact address, hours, and how to book. Most details are lost or hard to extract.

📈

Trust Gap in Traditional Platforms

77% of travelers look for friends' recommendations first. Traditional travel sites feel impersonal and inauthentic compared to creator content.

🎯

Craving Authentic Experiences

34% prioritize "authentic local experiences" post-pandemic. Cookie-cutter tourist itineraries no longer cut it for modern travelers.
