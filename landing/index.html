Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TikTok-to-Trip

How does the AI extract travel information?
Our AI uses advanced video analysis to process visual content, speech recognition to understand audio, and natural language processing to read captions and comments. It identifies locations, restaurants, hotels, activities, and timing to create comprehensive itineraries.
Which platforms are supported?
We currently support TikTok, Instagram Reels, Instagram posts, YouTube videos, and YouTube Shorts. We're actively working on adding support for Pinterest, Twitter/X videos, and Facebook content.
Can I edit the generated itinerary?
Absolutely! Every itinerary is fully customizable. You can add or remove activities, adjust timing, change dates, swap hotels, and personalize every aspect of your trip before booking.
Is it free to use?
We'll offer a free tier with limited extractions per month, plus premium plans for power users with unlimited extractions, advanced features, and priority support. Early waitlist members will get exclusive discounts!
When will TikTok-to-Trip launch?
We're currently in private beta with select users. We plan to open access to waitlist members in early 2026. Join the waitlist to be among the first to try it!
